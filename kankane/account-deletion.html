Account Deletion

Last Updated: July 25, 2025

1. Introduction

At Kankane Hotels and Restaurants ("we," "us," or "our"), we respect your right to control your personal information. This page outlines the process for requesting the deletion of your account and associated personal data from our mobile application and website (collectively, the "App").

2. How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and personal data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to kankanehotelsandrestaurants@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account.
  2. Include the following details in your email:
    • Your full name
    • The phone number linked to your account
    • A clear request to delete your account and personal data
  3. Alternatively, you can submit a deletion request directly via our designated account deletion URL: https://www.kankanehotelsandrestaurants.com/account-deletion
  4. Follow any additional instructions provided in our response to verify your identity and process your request.

3. What Happens After Your Request

  • Verification: We will verify your identity to ensure the request is legitimate. This may involve confirming details associated with your account.
  • Processing Time: Once verified, we will delete your account and associated personal data within 30 days, unless we are required to retain certain information for legal or operational purposes (e.g., compliance with tax or audit requirements).
  • Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
  • Data Retention: Some anonymized data may be retained for analytical purposes, but it will not be linked to you personally.

4. Impact of Account Deletion

Deleting your account will result in:

  • Permanent removal of your personal information, such as name, email, phone number, and order/booking history, from our systems (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Inability to access your account or use features requiring an account, such as placing new orders or bookings.
  • No impact on previously fulfilled orders or bookings, which may still be retained by restaurants or hotels as per their policies.
