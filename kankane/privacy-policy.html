Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Kankane Hotels and Restaurants ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "App") for food delivery and room booking services. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, delivery address, payment information, and guest details for room bookings provided when you create an account or place an order.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, such as pages visited, features used, and time spent on the App.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and browser type.
  • Location Data: With your consent, we may collect your location to facilitate food delivery or suggest nearby hotels.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill food delivery and room booking orders.
  • Communicate with you about your orders, account, or customer service inquiries.
  • Improve the App’s functionality and user experience.
  • Send promotional offers and updates, if you opt in to receive them.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or misuse of the App.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Restaurants, hotels, and delivery personnel to fulfill your orders.
  • Payment Processors: Third-party payment providers to process transactions securely.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.
  • Business Partners: With your consent, for marketing or promotional purposes.
We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices

You may:

  • Access or update your personal information through your account settings.
  • Opt out of promotional communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails.
  • Disable location services in your device settings, though this may limit some App features.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. When no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The App may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and collect usage data. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect the App’s functionality.

9. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

11. International Users

The App is operated from India. If you use the App from outside India, your information may be transferred to and processed in India, where data protection laws may differ. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on the App with the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
