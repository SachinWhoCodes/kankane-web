Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 25, 2025

1. Introduction

At Kankane Hotels and Restaurants ("we," "us," or "our"), we strive to ensure customer satisfaction with our food delivery and room booking services through our mobile application and website (collectively, the "App"). This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued for orders or bookings made through the App. Please read this policy carefully before using our services.

2. Food Delivery Refunds

Refunds for food delivery orders are subject to the following conditions:

  • Order Cancellation: You may cancel an order before it is confirmed by the restaurant. Once confirmed, cancellations are subject to the restaurant’s policy, which will be communicated at the time of ordering.
  • Non-Delivery: If an order is not delivered due to an error by us or the restaurant, you are eligible for a full refund.
  • Incorrect or Poor-Quality Food: If the delivered food is incorrect, damaged, or does not meet quality standards, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with details and, if possible, photographic evidence. Refunds or replacements will be processed at our discretion, in coordination with the restaurant.
  • Processing Time: Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

3. Room Booking Refunds

Refunds for room bookings are subject to the following conditions:

  • Cancellation by You: Refunds for cancellations are subject to the hotel’s cancellation policy, which will be provided at the time of booking. Some bookings may be non-refundable, as indicated during the reservation process.
  • Cancellation by Hotel: If a hotel cancels your booking or is unable to honor it due to unforeseen circumstances, you are eligible for a full refund or assistance in finding an alternative booking.
  • Modifications: Changes to booking dates or details are subject to availability and the hotel’s modification policy. Additional charges may apply, and no refunds will be issued for price differences unless specified by the hotel.
  • Processing Time: Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Failure to cancel within the restaurant’s or hotel’s specified cancellation window.
  • No-shows for room bookings without prior cancellation.
  • Issues arising from incorrect information provided by you, such as wrong delivery address or guest details.
  • Dissatisfaction due to personal taste preferences or minor delays beyond our control.

6. Disputes

If you are dissatisfied with the outcome of your refund request, you may escalate the matter by contacting us directly. We will review your case and respond within 7 business days. All refund decisions are final and subject to our discretion and the policies of the involved restaurant or hotel.

7. Changes to This Refund Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on the App with the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
